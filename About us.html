ABOUT TERRA REBELS

"Where Rebellion Meets Sustainability."

Terra Rebels is an NGO dedicated to empowering the youth of today to become active participants
in environmental protection, leadership development, and social impact.

Our Mission

At Terra Rebels we believe that youth
hold the power
to shape the future.

KEY MISSIONS

  • Lead initiatives focused on environmental sustainability - Encouraging youth to lead projects like tree planting, clean-up drives, and renewable energy
  • Educate communities about pressing environmental issues - Providing educational resources and programs on climate change, pollution, and resource
  • Foster a sense of responsibility toward nature - Helping youth build a lifelong connection to nature and encouraging eco-conscious habits.
  • Offer training, mentorship, and opportunities for hands-on experience - Providing youth with the skills they need to lead impactful environmental projects.

OUR CORE VALUES

At,Terra Rebels, we believe that youth hold the power to shape the future of our planet.We aim to equip youth with the tools, knowledge,
and hands-on experience to address pressing environmental issues and create a meaningful change in their communities.
