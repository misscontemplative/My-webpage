MAJOR WORKS AT TERRA REBELS

At Terra Rebels, we engage in several initiatives aimed at
creating a sustainable future. Here are some of the key programs and activities we are involved in:

ENVIRONMENTAL CAMPAINGS

We organize campaigns to raise awareness about pressing environmental issues like pollution, deforestation, and climate change. Through workshops, talks, and online campaigns, we aim to educate communities about the importance of environmental conservation.

LEADERSHIP TRAININGS

We offer leadership programs that equip young people with the skills needed to take charge of their own communities. Through workshops, mentoring, and leadership activities, we build the next generation of environmental leaders

CLEAN_UP CAMPAINGS

Our clean-up campaigns encourage youth and local communities to take action against littering and pollution. We organize regular clean-up drives in parks, beaches, and urban areas to create cleaner, healthier environments for all.

PARTNERSHIP

We collaborate with other NGOs, local governments, and community groups to expand the impact of our programs. By joining forces, we work together to address larger-scale environmental issues and create a lasting difference.

SUSTINABLE AGRICULTURAL WORKSHOPS

We offer hands-on training on sustainable farming methods, organic agriculture, and community-based farming to help youth and farmers adopt eco-friendly agricultural practices.

WILDLIFE CONSERVATION INITIATIVES

We partner with conservation groups and local authorities to protect endangered species and ecosystems. Our efforts include education, wildlife patrols, and habitat restoration.
